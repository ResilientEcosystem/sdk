Detailed Description

A Input is used to spend assets locked by an Output.

Wraps around a Crypto-condition Fulfillment.

fulfillment (:class:cryptoconditions.Fulfillment): A Fulfillment to be signed with a private key. owners_before (:obj:list of :obj:str): A list of owners after a Transaction was confirmed. fulfills (:class:~resdb.transaction. TransactionLink, optional): A link representing the input of a TRANSFER Transaction.
